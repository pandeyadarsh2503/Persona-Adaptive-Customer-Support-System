SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

Uptime Guarantee:
AdSpark guarantees 99.9% uptime per calendar month.

Downtime Definition:
Downtime refers to:
- API unavailability
- Dashboard inaccessibility
- Campaign serving failure

Compensation Policy:
If uptime falls below 99.9%:
- Eligible for service credit
- Credit calculated as % of monthly subscription fee

Scheduled Maintenance:
Scheduled maintenance windows are:
- Sundays 2 AM – 4 AM UTC
- Announced 48 hours in advance

Major Incidents:
For critical outages:
- Incident updates provided every 30 minutes
- Root cause analysis shared within 72 hours
