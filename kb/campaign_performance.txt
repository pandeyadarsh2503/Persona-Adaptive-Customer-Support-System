CAMPAIGN PERFORMANCE TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE — Campaign Manager Platform

=======================================================
SECTION 1: ADS NOT DELIVERING
=======================================================

If your campaign is active but ads are not being served, check the following in order:

1. Budget Status
   - Is the daily or total campaign budget exhausted? Check Campaign > Budget Status
   - If daily budget is depleted before midnight, ads stop serving until the next day
   - Solution: Increase daily budget or enable "Budget pacing" to distribute spend evenly throughout the day

2. Creative Approval Status
   - All ad creatives must pass our automated + manual review (24–48 hours for new ads)
   - Check Creative Library > Status column for: Approved, Pending Review, Disapproved
   - Disapproved creatives will not serve; fix the issue and resubmit for review

3. Audience Targeting Too Narrow
   - Very specific audience segments (high-intent, narrow geography, tight demographic) can result in zero or near-zero daily impressions
   - Expand targeting: broaden age range, add more interests, loosen geographic radius
   - Use Audience Insights to see estimated reach before launching

4. Bid Amount Too Low
   - In competitive auctions, bids below the floor price result in no wins
   - Check the Bid Recommendations widget on the campaign edit page for suggested bid ranges
   - Enable "Enhanced CPC" to let the system optimise bids automatically

5. Campaign Schedule Restrictions
   - Ads may be restricted to specific hours (dayparting) — verify Schedule settings
   - Ensure start date is not in the future and end date has not passed

6. Geo-targeting Conflict
   - Verify your target region has available inventory for your chosen ad format
   - Some geographies have limited Display or Video inventory; try expanding to regional or national level

=======================================================
SECTION 2: LOW IMPRESSIONS
=======================================================

Common causes and fixes:

Low Daily Impressions (under 1,000/day)
- Audience too small: Use Audience Estimator (reach must be >10,000 to compete in auctions)
- CPM bid too low: Increase target CPM bid by 20–30% and monitor impact
- Ad format restrictions: Video ads have fewer available placements than Display; consider adding Display format
- Frequency cap too strict: A cap of 1 impression per user per day significantly limits scale
- Negative keyword list too broad: Review and reduce negative keywords that may be blocking valid queries

Impressions Suddenly Dropped
- Check if any changes were made to the campaign in the past 24 hours (edits, budget changes, targeting changes)
- Review the Change History log: Campaign > History
- Check for a new policy enforcement action on your creatives
- Check our status page for any platform-wide performance issues

Improving Impression Volume:
1. Increase budget to remove the budget constraint
2. Widen audience targeting (add lookalike audiences)
3. Add more ad formats (Display + Video + Native)
4. Enable "Reach Maximiser" bidding strategy for awareness campaigns
5. Expand geographic targeting from city to state/country level

=======================================================
SECTION 3: LOW CLICK-THROUGH RATE (CTR)
=======================================================

Industry benchmark CTRs:
- Display ads: 0.05% – 0.1%
- Search ads: 2% – 5%
- Video ads (completion-based): 20%–35% view rate
- Native ads: 0.2% – 0.5%

Improving CTR:
- Use action-oriented headlines: "Get 50% Off Today" outperforms "Our Products"
- A/B test at least 3 creative variants per campaign simultaneously
- Ensure ad copy speaks to the audience's intent (match message to search query)
- Use ad extensions (site links, callouts, promotions) for Search campaigns
- Refresh creatives every 4–6 weeks to avoid ad fatigue
- Use dynamic keyword insertion for Search ads to increase relevance

=======================================================
SECTION 4: DISAPPROVED ADS
=======================================================

Top reasons for ad disapproval:
1. Prohibited content: Adult content, weapons, dangerous products, counterfeit goods
2. Misleading claims: Superlative claims ("Best in the world") without substantiation
3. Landing page issues: Landing page is down, loads slowly (<3 sec), or is inconsistent with ad copy
4. Trademark violations: Using competitor brand names in ad copy without authorisation
5. Grammar/spelling errors: Ads with obvious errors are rejected automatically
6. Circumventing systems: Using redirects or cloaking to hide final destination URL

How to fix a disapproved ad:
1. Go to Creative Library > click on disapproved ad > View Policy Reason
2. Read the detailed policy violation explanation
3. Edit the creative to resolve the issue
4. Click "Submit for Review" — review usually completes within 24 hours
5. Do not create duplicate ads to bypass review; this risks account suspension

Appealing a Disapproval:
- If you believe the disapproval is incorrect, click "Appeal" next to the ad
- Provide a clear explanation and any supporting documentation
- Appeals are reviewed by the Policy team within 48 business hours
- Approved appeals are valid for 30 days; resubmit if the creative changes

=======================================================
SECTION 5: CAMPAIGN OPTIMISATION BEST PRACTICES
=======================================================

Bidding Strategies:
- Target CPA: Best for conversion-focused campaigns; set target cost-per-acquisition
- Target ROAS: Optimise for return on ad spend; minimum 50 conversions/month recommended
- Maximise Conversions: Let the system spend budget for most conversions (no explicit CPA target)
- Manual CPC: Full control; recommended for experienced advertisers with clear data
- Enhanced CPC: Manual bids with automated adjustments for higher conversion probability

Conversion Tracking Setup:
1. Install the Campaign Manager pixel on your website (Settings > Tracking > Pixel Setup)
2. Create conversion events: Purchase, Lead Form Submission, Page View, Custom
3. Verify pixel is firing: Use the Tag Assistant browser extension
4. Allow 2 weeks of conversion data before enabling automated bidding strategies

Quality Score Improvement:
- Higher quality scores reduce your effective CPC
- Improve quality score by: matching ad copy to keywords, improving landing page relevance, increasing expected CTR
- Quality score is recalculated daily; changes reflect within 24 hours

=======================================================
SECTION 6: CONVERSION ISSUES
=======================================================

Conversions not being tracked:
- Verify the conversion pixel is installed on the confirmation/thank-you page, not the product page
- Check for ad blockers in your browser during testing (use incognito mode with extensions disabled)
- Cross-device conversions have a 7-day attribution window by default; adjust in Settings > Attribution
- View-through conversions are attributed within 24 hours of ad view
- Ensure the pixel fires on HTTPS pages only (mixed content blocks pixel loading)
