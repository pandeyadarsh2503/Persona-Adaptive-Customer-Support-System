CAMPAIGN PERFORMANCE ISSUES

Ads Not Delivering:
Possible causes:
- Insufficient budget
- Incorrect targeting
- Disapproved creatives

Low Impressions:
- Narrow audience targeting
- Low bid amount
- Campaign schedule restrictions

Budget Exhausted:
Campaign stops when daily or total budget is reached.
Increase budget from dashboard to resume.

Disapproved Ads:
Ads may be rejected due to:
- Policy violations
- Prohibited content
- Incorrect formatting

Review policy guidelines before resubmission.
