ANALYTICS & REPORTING

Real-Time Dashboard:
Campaign metrics update every 5 minutes.

Metrics Available:
- Impressions
- Clicks
- CTR
- Conversion rate
- Spend

Data Delay:
Analytics data may experience 2–5 minute delay.

Export Reports:
Users can export reports in:
- CSV
- PDF
- Excel format

API Reporting:
Reporting endpoints available via REST API.
Rate limit: 500 requests per minute.
