ACCOUNT & AUTHENTICATION SUPPORT

Password Reset:
Users can reset password using "Forgot Password" link.
Password reset link expires in 15 minutes.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
If user cannot access 2FA:
- Use backup codes.
- Contact support for identity verification.

Account Suspension:
Accounts may be suspended due to:
- Payment failure
- Policy violation
- Suspicious activity

Resolution:
- Clear pending invoices.
- Submit verification documents.
- Contact support for review.

Login Issues:
Ensure:
- Correct email address
- Browser cookies enabled
- No VPN blocking authentication
