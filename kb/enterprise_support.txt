=======================================================
SECTION 2: DEDICATED ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
=======================================================

Technical Account Manager (TAM):
- Your TAM is your primary technical point of contact
- Responsibilities: Onboarding, integration setup, API configuration, technical issue resolution
- TAM holds a dedicated 90-minute weekly check-in call (schedule adjustable by mutual agreement)
- Reachable via: Direct email, dedicated Slack channel, phone during business hours
- For Critical issues outside business hours: On-call TAM available via the Priority Hotline

Customer Success Manager (CSM):
- Your CSM is your strategic business point of contact
- Responsibilities: Account health monitoring, QBR facilitation, renewal negotiations, feature roadmap discussions
- CSM proactively monitors your campaign performance and alerts to optimisation opportunities
- Monthly business health report sent automatically on the first Monday of each month

Getting your TAM and CSM assigned:
- New Enterprise customers: TAM and CSM assigned within 2 business days of contract signing
- TAM introduction call scheduled within 1 week of onboarding kickoff
- Change requests for TAM/CSM: Contact enterprise@campaignmanager.io

Priority Support Channel:
- Enterprise customers receive a dedicated priority email queue: enterprise-support@campaignmanager.io
- Dedicated Slack workspace channel: #[your-company]-support (provisioned during onboarding)
- Priority Hotline (24/7 for Critical issues): +1-888-CM-PRIORITY (number provided in onboarding documents)

SECTION 3: ENTERPRISE SLA DETAILS
=======================================================

Standard Enterprise SLA (99.9% Uptime):
- Maximum allowed downtime: 43.8 minutes per calendar month
- First Response Time: Critical = 15 min, High = 2 hr, Medium = 8 hr, Low = 24 hr
- Resolution Time Target: Critical = 4 hr, High = 24 hr, Medium = 72 hr, Low = 5 business days

Premium Enterprise SLA (99.99% Uptime):
- Maximum allowed downtime: 4.38 minutes per calendar month
- First Response Time: Critical = 5 min, High = 1 hr, Medium = 4 hr, Low = 12 hr
- Resolution Time Target: Critical = 2 hr, High = 8 hr, Medium = 48 hr, Low = 3 business days

SLA Credits for Enterprise Customers:
- 99.5% – 99.9% uptime: 10% credit of monthly fee
- 99.0% – 99.5% uptime: 25% credit
- Below 99.0% uptime: 50% credit or financial penalty per contract terms

Financial Downtime Penalties (Premium SLA with Penalty Clause):
- Applicable when: Single incident >4 continuous hours OR cumulative >6 hours in a month
- Penalty = 1% of monthly contract value per hour of excess downtime, up to 100% of monthly fee
- Penalty payments issued within 30 days of verified claim approval

=======================================================
SECTION 4: QUARTERLY BUSINESS REVIEWS (QBRs)
=======================================================

QBR Schedule:
- Conducted every quarter (January, April, July, October)
- Standard enterprise: 90-minute virtual QBR
- Premium enterprise: In-person QBR option available at our offices or customer location

QBR Agenda (Typical):
1. Performance Review: Campaign metrics, ROI analysis, quarter-over-quarter trends
2. Platform Reliability: Uptime report, incident recap, any open SLA credit claims
3. Feature Roadmap: Upcoming platform features, beta program participation opportunities
4. Account Health: Billing review, user adoption, support ticket analysis
5. Strategic Planning: Goals for next quarter, optimisation recommendations
6. Action Items: Agreed timeline and owner for follow-ups

Requesting a QBR Reschedule:
- Minimum 5 business days notice required
- Contact your CSM directly via email or Slack

SECTION 5: CUSTOM INTEGRATIONS (ENTERPRISE)
=======================================================

Available Integrations:
- CRM Systems: Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics 365
- DSPs (Demand-Side Platforms): Trade Desk, DV360, AppNexus
- DMPs (Data Management Platforms): Oracle Audience Data, Salesforce DMP
- BI Tools: Tableau, Power BI, Looker (native connector + API)
- Marketing Automation: Marketo, Pardot, Eloqua
- Data Warehouses: Snowflake, BigQuery, Amazon Redshift (via data connector)
- Collaboration: Slack, Microsoft Teams (alerts and reporting notifications)

Custom Integration Setup:
1. Identify integration requirement during onboarding or in a dedicated requirements call with TAM
2. TAM scopes the integration (typically 2–4 weeks for standard integrations)
3. TAM provides integration documentation and API keys with appropriate scopes
4. Integration testing in sandbox environment (sandbox available to all Enterprise accounts)
5. Production go-live with TAM monitoring for first 7 days

Custom API Rate Limits:
- Standard Enterprise: Default 2,000 RPM; can increase to 5,000 RPM via request
- Premium Enterprise: Starts at 5,000 RPM; custom ceiling available on request
- Burst capacity: Up to 3x standard rate for 60-second bursts (traffic spikes)
- Rate limit increase requests processed within 2 business days

SECTION 6: DATA & SECURITY (ENTERPRISE)
=======================================================

Data Residency Options:
- US Region: AWS us-east-1 (Virginia) — default for US-based contracts
- EU Region: AWS eu-west-1 (Ireland) — required for GDPR-compliant contracts
- APAC Region: AWS ap-southeast-1 (Singapore)
- Cross-region replication can be disabled for data sovereignty requirements

Security Features (Enterprise):
- SAML 2.0 Single Sign-On with your IdP (Okta, Azure AD, Ping Identity, etc.)
- Advanced IP allowlisting: Restrict API and dashboard access to approved IP ranges
- Audit logs: Full user activity log retained for 12 months, exportable
- Custom data retention policy: Default 24 months; can be extended up to 7 years
- DPA (Data Processing Agreement): Available at legal@campaignmanager.io
- Penetration test results: Available under NDA for security teams

GDPR / CCPA Compliance:
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) signed for all EU Enterprise customers
- Right to Erasure requests: Submit via privacy@campaignmanager.io; processed within 30 days
- Data portability: Full data export in machine-readable format available within 5 business days
- Sub-processor list: Available at campaignmanager.io/legal/sub-processors
