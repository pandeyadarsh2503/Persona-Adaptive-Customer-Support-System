=======================================================
SECTION 2: BILLING CYCLE & INVOICES
=======================================================

Monthly Billing:
- Billing occurs on the same date each month (anniversary of subscription start)
- Payment is charged automatically to the saved payment method
- Invoices are generated 1 day before the billing date and emailed to the billing contact
- Download past invoices: Billing Dashboard > Invoice History > Download PDF

Annual Billing:
- Billed in full at the start of the annual period
- Cannot downgrade to monthly billing during the annual term
- Annual invoices include VAT/GST breakdown by region

Accepted Payment Methods:
- Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- ACH Bank Transfer (US customers, Enterprise plan only)
- Wire Transfer (Enterprise plan, minimum $5,000 annual contract)
- Invoiced Net-30 terms available for Enterprise contracts

Adding or Updating Payment Method:
1. Go to Billing > Payment Methods
2. Click "Add New Payment Method" or "Edit" on existing
3. Card details are stored via PCI-DSS compliant tokenisation (we never store raw card numbers)
4. Set new card as default for future charges

=======================================================
SECTION 3: REFUND POLICY
=======================================================

Monthly Subscriptions:
- Full refund available if requested within 7 days of the billing date
- No partial refunds for unused days beyond the 7-day window
- Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days to the original payment method

Annual Subscriptions:
- Refund within 14 days of annual billing date: Full refund minus any overage usage charges
- After 14 days: Prorated refund calculated based on remaining full months unused (no partial month refunds)
- The minimum non-refundable amount is 2 months of equivalent monthly cost

Enterprise Contracts:
- Refunds governed by the specific terms in your contract
- Contact your account manager or legal@campaignmanager.io for contract-specific refund terms

Overage Charges:
- Overage on impressions: $0.50 per 1,000 impressions above plan limit (CPM)
- Overage charges are billed at the end of the billing cycle, not in real-time
- Set up overage alerts in Billing > Notifications to receive warnings at 80% and 95% of plan limits
- You can set a hard cap to stop serving ads when limit is reached (no overage charges)

=======================================================
SECTION 4: PAYMENT FAILURE HANDLING
=======================================================

What happens when payment fails:
1. First failure: Immediate retry attempted 24 hours after failure
2. Second failure: Retry after 3 days; warning email sent to billing contact
3. Third failure: Account enters 7-day grace period, services continue
4. End of grace period: Campaign serving is paused; account access for data export only
5. After 30 days with no payment: Account is archived (data retained for 90 days then deleted)

Resolving a payment failure:
1. Go to Billing > Outstanding Balance
2. Review the failed charge amount and reason (insufficient funds, card declined, expired card)
3. Update your payment method if needed
4. Click "Retry Payment" to immediately attempt charge on new/updated card
5. Services resume within 15 minutes of successful payment

VAT / Tax Handling:
- EU customers: VAT is added based on country of billing address
- US customers: Sales tax applied in applicable states (NY, TX, CA, etc.)
- Provide your VAT registration number in Billing > Tax Settings to receive invoices without VAT (for B2B in EU)
- Non-profit discounts: 20% discount available with proof of 501(c)(3) or equivalent

=======================================================
SECTION 5: PLAN UPGRADES & DOWNGRADES
=======================================================

Upgrading Plan:
- Effective immediately; prorated credit for remaining days of current plan applied to next invoice
- Access to new features begins instantly after upgrade confirmation

Downgrading Plan:
- Effective at the end of the current billing cycle
- Cannot downgrade if current usage exceeds the lower plan's limits (e.g., more than 5 active campaigns on Starter)
- Reduce campaigns to within the plan limit before requesting downgrade

Cancelling Subscription:
- Cancel from Billing > Subscription > Cancel Plan
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period
- Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation; export your data before this window closes
- Reactivation within 90 days restores all historical data; after 90 days, data is permanently deleted
