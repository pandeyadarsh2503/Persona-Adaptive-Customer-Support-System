ESCALATION POLICY

Escalation Conditions:
Cases escalated to human agent if:
- Refund demand
- Legal threat
- Enterprise customer complaint
- Repeated unresolved issue
- Explicit request for human support

Priority Classification:

Low:
General queries, informational requests.

Medium:
Technical issues affecting limited functionality.

High:
Repeated failures, billing disputes, enterprise impact.

Critical:
System-wide outage, legal threat, enterprise SLA breach.

Response Time Targets:
Low: 24 hours
Medium: 8 hours
High: 2 hours
Critical: Immediate
